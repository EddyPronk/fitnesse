!3 !-!contents-! with REGRACE_TOC variable
'''Use !contents to list all the child pages of the current page. The "-g" option will display graceful names in the list. You can also use the "REGRACE_TOC" variable to "re-grace" or "spacify" the run-on names (see other Test!--!Contents* pages)'''

|Action Fixture.|
|start|Page Builder|
|enter|line|I'm the parent|
|enter|line|!-!define REGRACE_TOC {true}-!|
|enter|line|!-!contents-!|
|enter|line|!-!define REGRACE_TOC {false}-!|
|enter|page|!-ParentPage-!|

!include -seamless ContentsTestsInclude

'''Then request the parent page.'''
|Response Requester.|
|uri|valid?|contents?|
|!-ParentPage-!|true||
